About Me

I am currently a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) Bangalore Centre, working with Prof. Jaydeb Sarkar. Previously, I served as an Institute Post-Doctoral Fellow and a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay.

Research Interests

My research interests are centered around Functional Analysis, Complex Analysis, Operator Theory, and Function Theory, with a particular emphasis on:

  • Dilations & Commutant Lifting
  • Toeplitz & Hankel Operators
  • Complete Nevanlinna-Pick Spaces
  • de Branges-Rovnyak Spaces
  • Non-commutative Function Theory
